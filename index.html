Norton Hall, Northamptonshire

A press based in Northamptonshire, continuing the tradition of private scholarship. Norton Hall publishes works in ontology, cosmology, and awareness. Its public works are given freely. The Circular is its weekly voice.

Publications

Vol. I

Necessitas

On the Conditions Under Which Anything Is the Case

A structural account of what must hold for anything to obtain. Beginning from the single recognition that something is the case, Necessitas derives the minimal conditions under which any reality — any world — can exist at all.
