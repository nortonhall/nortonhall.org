What Norton Hall Does

A small independent press based in Northamptonshire continuing the older tradition of private scholarship and printing. Norton Hall publishes works concerned with ontology, cosmology, and the conditions under which awareness and meaning become possible. Its publishing activity takes several forms: formal printed volumes issued in small runs, a monthly periodical known as The Circular, and a growing body of selected writings made publicly available online. Public Works are given freely, forming a shared archive of essays, letters, and reflections drawn from the wider body of material produced through the press. Together these form an ongoing inquiry carried out in public, allowing readers not only to encounter finished work but to follow the development of the ideas themselves.
